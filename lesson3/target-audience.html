Target Audience

The target audience of the Spirit of Enterprise is local business owners, entrepreneurs and those worked in a variety fields including retail sales, hospitality, financial services, professional services and manufacturing. We seek to provide support for small businesses by advocating on their behalf with state and federal governments and connecting them with resources that can help grow their operations. We also serve as an information source regarding essential economic updates from both private-sector trends within the community as well its public counterpart at municipal or county levels. Additionally we cultivate relationships between our members through networking events held semi regularly which allow individuals working in different industries to learn more about what new opportunities are available throughout their region.
