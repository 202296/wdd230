Purpose

The purpose of Abidjan Chamber of Commerce is to create an environment where businesses have the opportunity to connect, collaborate, and grow together. We strive to promote economic development while also helping local business owners succeed. Our ultimate goal is for members to not just survive but thrive in their respective markets. Abidjan Chamber of Commerce provide support services such as networking events, educational workshops, seminars; lobbying government officials on behalf of its members' interests; partnering with other organizations for collective-marketing efforts and foster an environment where businesses can prosper and be part of economic growth.
