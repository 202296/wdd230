Typography

Heading Font: Roboto

I deceided to used Roboto for my Headline because it is a sans-serif font family, suitable and make my headlines look strong and inviting.

Paragraph Font: Open Sans

I diceided to used Open Sans for my Paragraph because it is easy to read and have a friendly appearance and it is very attractive.

Normal paragraph example

A chamber of commerce is an association made up of individuals and businesses who work together to promote the economic development, growth, and success of their local area. Chambers typically use networking opportunities like trade shows and business-to-business meetings to bring members from different industries into contact with each other. They also advocate for pro-business policy initiatives at a local or regional level, such as favorable tax codes that incentivize businesses in order to encourage job creation and overall financial security within the community.
