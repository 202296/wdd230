Our Story

Bountiful Foods was founded in 2014 with the mission to make high-quality, farm-fresh fruits more accessible to everyone, and was the first company to successfully ship fruits to homes across the country. Since 2019 Bountiful FoodsRx has been disrupting the healthcare industry. As an extension of the Bountiful Foods Direct mission, Bountiful FoodsRx focuses on proactive wellness by delivering food as medicine across America in partnership with Medicaid and Medicare programs. Bountiful FoodsRx is a first-of-its-kind nutrition program that makes healthy eating accessible to members of wellness-focused health insurers. Bountiful FoodsRx may be available through your Medicaid or Medicare program as an Advantage Benefit, part of your Over the Counter (OTC) Card benefit, or as a part of your Healthy Food Card benefit.

Bountiful Foods Direct, the parent company of Bountiful FoodsRx, was created by Ashley Tyrner with a clear vision that healthy foods should be accessible to all. Beginning as a local delivery service in NYC, the company quickly expanded to meet the demands of the millions of Americans living in food deserts who lack access to fresh fruits.
